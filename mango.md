# Mango - The King of Fruits

Mangoes are tropical stone fruits belonging to the genus Mangifera, originating from South Asia over 4,000 years ago and now cultivated in warm climates worldwide. These sweet, aromatic fruits are rich in vitamins A and C, dietary fiber, potassium, and powerful antioxidants like mangiferin, making them both delicious and nutritious with numerous health benefits including immune system support and improved digestion.
