# Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. This document profiles some of the most notable batsmen and bowlers in cricket history.

## Batsman Profiles

### Sachin Tendulkar

**Basic Info:**
- Country: India
- Born: April 24, 1973
- Batting Style: Right-handed
- Role: Top-order batsman

**Statistics:**

| Format | Matches | Runs | Average | Strike Rate |
|--------|---------|------|---------|-------------|
| Test | 200 | 15,921 | 53.78 | 54.08 |
| ODI | 463 | 18,426 | 44.83 | 86.23 |
| T20I | 1 | 10 | 10.00 | 71.42 |

**Playing Style and Strengths:**
- Master of all formats with exceptional technique
- Impeccable timing and placement
- Strong on both front and back foot
- Ability to adapt to different conditions and bowling attacks
- Legendary straight drives and cover drives

**Notable Achievements and Records:**
- Only player to score 100 international centuries
- Highest run-scorer in both Test and ODI cricket
- First batsman to score a double century in ODI cricket
- Played 664 international matches over 24 years

### Virat Kohli

**Basic Info:**
- Country: India
- Born: November 5, 1988
- Batting Style: Right-handed
- Role: Top-order batsman

**Statistics:**

| Format | Matches | Runs | Average | Strike Rate |
|--------|---------|------|---------|-------------|
| Test | 113 | 8,848 | 49.15 | 55.47 |
| ODI | 295 | 13,906 | 58.18 | 93.54 |
| T20I | 125 | 4,188 | 52.73 | 137.04 |

**Playing Style and Strengths:**
- Aggressive and consistent run-scorer
- Exceptional chaser in limited-overs cricket
- Master of running between wickets
- Strong cover drives and flicks through mid-wicket
- Excellent fitness and intensity

**Notable Achievements and Records:**
- Fastest to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, 12,000, and 13,000 runs in ODI cricket
- Holds record for most centuries while chasing in ODIs
- Former captain of Indian cricket team across all formats
- 80 international centuries across all formats

### Sir Don Bradman

**Basic Info:**
- Country: Australia
- Born: August 27, 1908
- Batting Style: Right-handed
- Role: Middle-order batsman

**Statistics:**

| Format | Matches | Runs | Average | Strike Rate |
|--------|---------|------|---------|-------------|
| Test | 52 | 6,996 | 99.94 | 60+ (estimated) |
| First-class | 234 | 28,067 | 95.14 | N/A |

**Playing Style and Strengths:**
- Greatest batsman of all time with unmatched consistency
- Exceptional concentration and technique
- Innovative footwork and shot selection
- Ability to score quickly while maintaining control
- Devastating pull shot and precise placement

**Notable Achievements and Records:**
- Test batting average of 99.94, widely considered unbreakable
- 29 Test centuries from just 52 matches
- Scored centuries in 33.9% of all innings played
- Made 12 double centuries and 2 triple centuries in Tests

### Brian Lara

**Basic Info:**
- Country: West Indies
- Born: May 2, 1969
- Batting Style: Left-handed
- Role: Top-order batsman

**Statistics:**

| Format | Matches | Runs | Average | Strike Rate |
|--------|---------|------|---------|-------------|
| Test | 131 | 11,953 | 52.88 | 60.51 |
| ODI | 299 | 10,405 | 40.48 | 79.51 |

**Playing Style and Strengths:**
- Elegant stroke-maker with immense natural talent
- Dominant against pace and spin bowling
- Exceptional wrist work and timing
- Ability to play long innings and big scores
- Signature cover drives and flicks through leg side

**Notable Achievements and Records:**
- Holds record for highest individual Test score: 400 not out
- Holds record for highest first-class score: 501 not out
- Only player to reclaim the record for highest Test score
- 53 international centuries across Test and ODI formats
